My arts and crafts mediums:

Colouring

In art, "medium" refers to the substance the artist uses to create a piece of artwork. I other words: pens and papers etc:.

Colours:

I use an assortement of different colouring pencils, watercolour pencils, pens, alcohol markers and gellrolls.
My favourites and most used are Prismacolor Premier they are a bit pricey but divine. A cheaper alernative would be my Derwent coloursoft.

Papers:

I mostly use adult colouringbooks, sometimes kids colouringbooks. But i have several scetchpads for my scetches (these wont be available to see here).
My most used books are the ones by Hanna Karlzon and CAMILLA d'ERICO, these amazing artist needs more attention.

Blending:

Blending is used to make the colours seamless join together. There are several different ways to do this.
The way i prefer is blending solvent or a specific blending pen. You can use baby or any kind of oils too but it's not as easy and might leave a stain.

Jewellery:

I have tons of materials for al different kinds of jewellery. Bracelets are my favourites but earrings can be a lot of fun too.
I went to a goldsmith to learn to do queen- and kingschains properly. It takes several hours for each bracelet but the results are gorgeous.
I also make beadbracelets with all kinds of beads, plastic, gemstones, metal, wood, and letters. My favouritesare my curse bracelets.

Soap:

I use melt and pour kind of soap as i don't think i should be allowed to play with chemicals.
Then i use one of 10 colours to get the one i want and lastly I add my scents. I have about 30 different etherical oils to always get it right.
