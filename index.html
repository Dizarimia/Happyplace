My arts and crafts mediums:

Mediums:

In art, "medium" refers to the substance the artist uses to create a piece of artwork. I other words: pens and papers etc:.

Colours:

I use an assortement of different colouring pencils, watercolour pencils, pens, alcohol markers and gellrolls. My favourites and most used are Prismacolor Premier they are a bit pricey but divine. A cheaper alernative would be my Derwent coloursoft.

Papers:

I mostly use adult colouringbooks, sometimes kids colouringbooks. But i have several scetchpads for my scetches (these wont be available to see here). My most used books are the ones by Hanna Karlzon and CAMILLA d'ERICO, these amazing artist needs more attention.

Blending:

Blending is used to make the colours seamless join together. There are several different ways to do this. The way i prefer is blending solvent or a specific blending pen. You can use baby or any kind of oils too but it's not as easy and might leave a stain.
